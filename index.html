Fewa lake is located in South east Asian country in nepal's Kaski district. A famous place of Pokhara valley was called Baidan Tal in previous. It is second largest lake of Nepal and biggest lake of Gandaki province. Fewa lake is located in an altitude of 742 m and it covers the area around 4.43km square. The lake is famous for reflection of mount Machhapuchhre due to it's clear water. The tal Barahi also located in middle of lake's island.
